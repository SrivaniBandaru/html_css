Sai Pallavi

Sai Pallavi Senthamarai Kannan is an Indian actress who mainly works in Tamil, Telugu and Malayalam films. She is the recipient of numerous accolades, including four Filmfare Awards South and two South Indian International Movie Awards. She was featured by Forbes magazine as one of India's 30 under 30 in 2020.
